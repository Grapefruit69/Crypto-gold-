# Gold & Crypto: The New Age of Value

## üåç Introduction

Gold has been the ultimate store of value for thousands of years. In contrast, cryptocurrencies are a digital innovation of the last two decades. Both represent alternative assets outside of traditional banking, but their roles, risks, and opportunities are very different ‚Äî and increasingly connected.

This repository explores the relationship between **gold and crypto**, their similarities, differences, and the evolving landscape where physical and digital value intersect.
